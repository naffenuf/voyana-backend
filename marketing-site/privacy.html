Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

Your privacy is important. This policy outlines the handling of data collected through the walking tour application (the "App").

Information Collection and Use

  • No user registration or personally identifiable information is collected.
  • We collect anonymized analytics and crash reports via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics for app improvement.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or disclose analytics data to third parties except through Firebase services, which handle data anonymously.

Location Information

  • Location data is accessed only when actively navigating a tour to provide accurate directions and relevant audio content.
  • Location data is not stored, shared, or tracked beyond your immediate usage.

Advertising and Tracking

  • The App does not contain advertisements or tracking requiring Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent.

Data Security

  • Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect anonymized data collected through the App.

Children’s Privacy

  • The App does not intentionally collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this Policy

  • Changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated within the App.

Contact

  • For privacy questions or concerns, please contact us at info@voyanatours.com.
